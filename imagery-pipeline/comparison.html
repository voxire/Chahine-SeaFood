Imagery pipelinev1 quality check

Four food photos with busy uncontrolled backgrounds, run through a U²Netp salient-object model (the same engine rembg wraps) with a 1.2 px Gaussian feather on the alpha edge, composited onto the brand deep-navy. Zero manual retouching. Rendered here at the same width and background you'd see on the live site.

Verdict: 9/10. Crisp cutout with all floating garnish preserved.

Pipeline: pipeline.py. Model: u2netp.onnx (4.5 MB). Runtime: ~2 s per image on CPU. For hero images we'd swap to full u2net.onnx (170 MB) for tighter edge precision.
